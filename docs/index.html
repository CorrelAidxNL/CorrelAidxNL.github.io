Good Causes. Better Effects.

CorrelAid Netherlands is a non-partisan, non-profit network of data scientists who want to use their skills to advance the social good.

Latest Projects

View all

Our core aim is to connect data scientists and data enthusiasts with organisations that advance the social good, but which may lack the data skills to best further their goals.

CrowdFight

Helps improve the profiles of CrowdFight volunteers and classify the incoming requests based on their scientific domain.

Sustainability Leadership Kosova

Used data to influence an ongoing governmental draft on deforestation regulation strategy as well as empower civil groups to increase pressure on the government to act.

Simavi

Analyzed baseline and midline surveys that measure the impact of Simavi's WASH programme in three different countries, specifically focusing on Gender Equality and Social Inclusion.
