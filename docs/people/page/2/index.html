People

Over the years, CorrelAid Netherlands has been blessed with many enthusiastic volunteers. This has included both those in the core-team / crew that have helped run the organisation, as well as those participating in our individual data for good projects. On this page you can find profiles of such volunteers, both past and present. If you would like to join their number, then head over here and get in touch! If you are a (former) volunteer and would like your profile here, please contact Alexis (alexis@correlaid.nl).
