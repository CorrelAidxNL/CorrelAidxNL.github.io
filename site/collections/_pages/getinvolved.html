As a volunteer

Do you want to apply your data science skills to real world problems? Then CorrelAid Netherlands is for you! In teams of usually 2-4 people, we help NGOs gain insight into their own data and use the results.

We announce projects on Twitter, Facebook, LinkedIn, through our Newsletter, and on Slack, so be sure to follow us there. If you want to join our community on Slack, please fill out this form. Afterwards, we will contact you to have a chat and invite you. If you have more questions, please contact Alexis.

Are you working on an interesting data project and want to share your results with a wider audience? Is your organisation using data in innovative ways to tackle difficult societal challenges? Do you have skills that you want to teach others? Then write a blog post for us, speak at one of our Meetups, or teach a workshop. If you are interested, please contact Fabian.

As an NGO

Are you part of an NGO that wants to use data science to operate more efficiently and effectively? Do you already have data and are looking for skilled analysts to get insights out of them? Or are you simply looking for somebody to brainstorm with you about what is possible with data and data science? Regardless of what you need, we are happy to help! Please contact Mehrzad to schedule a call.
