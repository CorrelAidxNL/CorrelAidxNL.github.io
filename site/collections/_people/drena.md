Drena joined CorrelAid in 2020 with an aim to help impact-driven organisations make better use of their data.

and in her work she's focused on using data to make more sustainable investments in developing countries.
Drena's passionate about data and development economics and has 10+ years of experience in the field including areas of
impact investing, public policy, social entrepreneurship, and private sector development.
At CorrelAid, Drena primarily focuses on the engagement of new organisations for projects and on community building.
